The Magic Behind the Vibe.fun Pump

Fair Launch Phase

Our token distribution kicks off with a 20-minute fair launch phase, where tokens are sold at a fixed price. This prevents large investors from monopolizing the supply and ensures that everyone has an equal opportunity to get in early. As soon as you purchase 1% of the total supply, you automatically receive an exclusive NFT tied to the project.

NFT Integration

Each NFT is a unique, collectible asset tied to the token sale. For every 1% of the total supply purchased, an NFT is generated and distributed. With a total of 100 NFTs available, these tokens add both value and scarcity to the ecosystem.

Transaction Fee Distribution

Every time Vibe.fun tokens are traded, a transaction fee is applied. This fee is distributed in a way that benefits the entire ecosystem.

  • 10% goes to the token creators.
  • 5% is distributed to NFT holders, providing additional rewards for loyal supporters.
  • 80% is used for buybacks, helping to increase liquidity and support the token's value in the market.
  • 5% is allocated to the platform treasury, ensuring the long-term growth and stability of the project.

Automatic Fee Trigger

To maintain smooth operation, when transaction fees reach 0.1% of the token's market value, the system automatically triggers the fee distribution mechanism, ensuring that funds are allocated efficiently and without manual intervention.

Curve Binding Pricing

After the fair launch phase, Vibe.fun transitions to a dynamic pricing model based on market demand. The price adjusts automatically according to a curve, ensuring a stable and balanced market while avoiding drastic price fluctuations. This model keeps the token's value resilient and adaptable, maintaining a healthy ecosystem for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about Vibe.fun and how it works.

What is Vibe.fun?

Vibe.fun is an innovative crypto project that integrates fair token launches, NFTs, and dynamic tokenomics to create a unique and sustainable ecosystem. It offers users the chance to participate in a 20-minute fair launch, earn exclusive NFTs with token purchases, and benefit from transaction fee distribution and buybacks that help maintain the stability of the token.

How does the fair launch work?

The fair launch phase lasts for 20 minutes, during which tokens are sold at a fixed price, ensuring equal opportunity for all participants. This eliminates the possibility of whales or early investors taking advantage of discounted prices. Additionally, each time a user purchases 1% of the total token supply during this phase, they receive a unique NFT, adding an extra layer of value to their investment.

How are the NFTs related to the project?

NFTs issued during the exhibition are directly related to the project and serve as collectibles that reflect your participation in the project. Each NFT is generated uniquely, and its value will increase as the project progresses. They have scarcity and potential utility within the ecosystem.

How are the transaction fees allocated?

Each transaction involving $VIBE tokens includes a fee, which is distributed to support the project and its community. A portion of the fee rewards token creators and NFT holders, while the majority is used for buybacks to ensure liquidity and price stability. A smaller portion goes into the platform treasury to support ongoing development.

What is the buyback mechanism?

The buyback mechanism uses 80% of transaction fees to purchase tokens from the open market, which increases liquidity and helps support the value of the token. This mechanism plays a key role in ensuring long-term price stability and market health.

What is the curve binding pricing model?

After the fair launch, Vibe.fun transitions to a dynamic pricing model known as curve binding. This model automatically adjusts the token price based on market demand, ensuring that prices remain stable and preventing sudden fluctuations. The curve binding system helps create a more balanced and sustainable market environment.

Can I participate in the project without purchasing tokens?

While purchasing $VIBE tokens gives you full access to the benefits of the project, such as earning NFTs, staking rewards, and participating in governance, you can still stay involved by engaging with the community, spreading the word, and supporting the project's growth.

What are the benefits of holding $VIBE?

Owning $VIBE tokens provides multiple benefits, including the ability to participate in the fair launch, earn NFTs, and receive passive rewards through staking and transaction fee distributions. Token holders can also participate in governance, allowing them to vote on key decisions that shape the project's future.
